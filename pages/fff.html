3D PRINTER

There are many types and brands of printers, we use a Creality CR6-SE. That means it is a desktop production ready 3D Printer. The extruder moves left and right as well as up and down. The table moves forwards and backwards. Between those three movement directions, all of the necessary motions are able to be performed. The part is formed by extruded softened plastic into beads on the print bed, and slowly building up layer by layer into the final shape.

FILAMENT

FFF or FDM 3D printing requires the use of filament. This is plastic or polymers or polymer composites that have been spun orextruded into longs strands or filaments. These come on spools where they are stored or feed into the machine from. We use four (4) different types of filament: PLA, PETG, PLA+CF, and PETG+CF. Check out the following tables to be able to read more about what each filament is good for and offers your parts.

OPTIONS

Filament Application Colors Pros Cons Delay
PLA (PolyLactic Acid) Great for looking vivid prints. Low use and low strength parts Black, White, Grey, and Blue Great for beautiful parts. Very easily painted Not the strongest. Does not stand up in the elements 2-3 days
PETG (PolyEthylene Terephthalate Glycol) Great for low use functional parts. Low use and high strength parts. Black, White, Grey, and Blue Stronger than PLA. Stands up in the elements Very difficult to paint. Can have surface iregularities 3-4 days
PLA+CF (PolyLactic Acid and Carbon Fiber Filler) Great for beautiful parts and prototypes. High use and low strength parts. Black and Grey Stronger than PLA, more ridgid than PLA, and great dimentionality. A beautiful carbon fiber finish. Lower detail than regular PLA. Has longer print times. 2-3 days
PETG+CF (PolyEthylene Terephthalate Glycol and Carbon Fiber Filler) Strong functional parts and prototypes. High use and high strength parts. Black and Grey Stronger than PETG, more ridgid than PETG, and great dimentionality. A beautiful carbon fiber finish. Lowest detail than regular PETG. Has extremely long print times. 3-4 days
