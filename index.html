FFF 3D PRINTING

FFF (also called FDM) is a 3D printing style where plastic (in the form of a filament) is extruded or deposited in a small bead layer by layer. The final shape is the colmunations of those layers. Filament available in PLA, PETG, PLA+CF, & PETG+CF. Different colors are available for PLA & PETG.
